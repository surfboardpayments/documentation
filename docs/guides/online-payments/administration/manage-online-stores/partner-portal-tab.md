---
title: "Create and Update Online Store Using Partner Portal"
description: "Guide for create and update online stores (Create or Update) Using Partner Portal."
category: "Administration"
path: "/guides/online-payments/administration/manage-online-stores/partner-portal-tab.md"
---

1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, go to the **Merchants** tab, and select the desired merchant.
2. In the **Stores** section, click **Edit** next to the store, then select **Edit Store Details**.
3. Update the necessary fields to mark it as an online store and click **Update Store**.
