# Merchant Portal

## Overview

The Merchant Portal is a comprehensive platform designed to help you manage your business operations, track sales, and configure your payment settings and much more. This guide will walk you through each section of the portal to help you maximize its functionality.

## Dashboard

In the home page, the dashboard provides a quick snapshot of your business performance as you can take a look below

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/dashboard.png "merchant portal")

### Key Metrics

**Home**

**Business overview**

- **Total Sales**: You can see your monthly total revenue
- **Number of Sales**: It shows you total transactions for the current and previous month
- **Average Order Value**: Mean transaction amount is displayed here.

### Features

- **Sales Graph**: Visual representation of product sales for better insights.
- **Detailed View**: Click on individual sales for comprehensive transaction details.

## Sidebar

The dashboard section in the sidebar offers you several features that enables you to access the respective details as follows

### **Sales**

This section provides you a detailed transaction history.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/sales.png "merchant portal")

**Initiate Refund for a transaction**

You can also initiate refund for a particular transaction in this section. To do that, 

1. Simply click the transaction you want to refund in sales section
2. Click **Create refund** 
3. **Initiate refund** in the pop up sheet from the right.

![Refunds](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/initiate-refund.png "merchant portal")

### **Reports**

Comprehensive financial reporting can be accessed here such as 

- Settlement date
- Transaction dates
- Report type
- Transaction ID

You can search for a specific transaction by free search or search by option and also can download a detailed Z-Day report for any transaction you want.

![Reports](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/reports.png "merchant portal")

### **Terminals**

This section allows you to register new terminal and see all the active terminals and details associated with it.

![Terminals](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/terminals.png "merchant portal")

**To register a new terminal**

1. Click **+ Register new terminal** at the top right corner.
2. Enter the Terminal name. Registration ID and select the store under which you wish to register the terminal and click **Register new terminal.**

![new terminal](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/register-new-terminal.png "merchant portal")

You also have options to configure additional settings for a particular terminal by clicking the arrow in the right corner of a particular terminal.

- **Transactions**: You can **create a refund** for a transaction made in the terminal here by selecting a particular transaction.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/transactions.png "merchant portal")

- **Settings**: Here you have an option to **change store** or **deactivate terminal**

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/terminal-settings.png "merchant portal")

- **Tips**: You can configure the tips settings in a terminal

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/terminal-tips.png "merchant portal")

- **Info**: You can see the terminal info in this section

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/terminal-info.png "merchant portal")

### **Stores**

All the store-related configurations are available here, where you can create a new store or select any available store to adjust its settings.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/stores.png "merchant portal")

**To Create a new store**

1. Click **+ create new store** in the top right corner.
2. Enter the store details and click **Create new** at the bottom right corner to create a new store.

**Note**: If you wish to enable online payments for your store, you can enter the online store information. Otherwise, skip it and proceed further.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/create-new-store.png "merchant portal")

You also have the flexibility to select any store in the store section and adjust the following settings associated with the store.

1. **Terminals:** You can modify the settings of the terminal linked to the selected store here.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/terminals-stores.png "merchant portal")

2. **Store branding:** This section allows you to customize the store’s branding options, enabling you to modify elements such as logos, colors, and themes to align with your brand identity.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/store-branding.png "merchant portal")

3. **Store Settings:** Here you can see the store details, update information like name, email, etc., and also modify store settings to enable online payments with required options.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/store-settings.png "merchant portal")

4. **Tips:** You can configure tip settings at the store level by enabling the toggle switch and adjusting the preferences accordingly.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/tips.png "merchant portal")

### **Products**

In this section you can create or add products to your store. To do that,

1. Click **Create new products**
2. Fill in the product details you require to add and create new product.

![Producsts](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/products.png "merchant portal")

### **Payment Links**

You can manage your online payment links in this section.

![Payment links](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/payment-links.png "merchant portal")

**Activate a Payment Page for a Store:** If you have enabled online payments for your store, you can register payment link as an online terminal for your store. To do that,

1. Select a store form the list of online store in the payment links section.
2. Click **Add Payment page Terminal**
3. Select **Register New Terminal** to register a new payment page terminal.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/activate-payment-links.png "merchant portal")

**Generate payment links**

1. Select an online store where you have registered a payment page terminal.
2. Click the online terminal for which you want to generate payment links.
3. Fill in the necessary fields, and click **Create Link.**
4. Share the link with your customer to accept online payments.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/generate-payment-links.png "merchant portal")

### **Order tracking and management**

Here, you can view the status for all **online orders** linked to the merchant.

![order tracking](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/orders.png "merchant portal")

You can view the details of a specific online order by clicking the arrow in the right corner of the order.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/track-orders.png "merchant portal")


>Payment Links and Orders sections are visible to merchants who have enabled online payment acceptance.


### Settings

This section provides you the following options,

**Profile details:** Merchant information is displayed in this section

![Merchant settings](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/merchant-setting.png "merchant portal")

**Company details and configurations:** You can see the company details and also configure the merchant level branding such as logo type, shape, background colour, etc,.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/merchant-info.png "merchant portal")

**Payment methods**: Here you can activate and deactivate payment methods as needed.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/payment-methods.png "merchant portal")

### Contracts

You can manage your business agreements through our Contracts section.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/contracts.png "merchant portal")

### **Disputes**

This section allows you, as a merchant, to submit dispute details, upload supporting documents, and provide a description to help resolve customer disputes efficiently.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/disputes.png "merchant portal")

### **Support**

This section provides a chatbot assistant, categorized FAQs, and troubleshooting resources to help you resolve issues efficiently.

![Dashboard](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/merchant-portal/support.png "merchant portal")
