---
title: "Online Terminal Registration"
description: "Guide to registering online terminals using partner portal."
category: "Online Payments"
path: "/guides/online-payments/terminals-registration/register-partner-portal-tab.md"
---

1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, navigate to **Merchants** tab and select the desired merchant.
2. In the merchant's page, go to the **Stores** section and choose the relevant store.
3. Click **Register Terminal**.
4. Select the **Register Online Terminal** from the dropdown, enter the required details, and complete the terminal registration.
