You can also initiate refund for a particular transaction in the merchant portal. To do that,

1. Login to the merchant portal, go to **Sales** section in the side bar, Simply click the **Transaction** you want to refund in sales section.
2. Click **Create refund**
3. **Initiate refund** in the pop up sheet from the right.

{% callout type="note" label="Note" %}
Alternative: In side bar, Go to Terminal section, You can create a refund for a particular transaction by clicking the arrow in the right corner.
{% /callout %}
