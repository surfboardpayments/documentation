## Let's Get Your First Payments Done

Congratulations on setting up your merchant account! This guide covers all the essential steps to help you process your first payment successfully and smoothly.

Throughout this guide, you will learn how to:

1. **Terminal Registration**: Register and activate a terminal via either Surfboard APIs or the Partner Portal.
2. **Create Order**: Create an order for a transaction.
3. **Initiate a Payment**: Begin the payment process for an order.
4. **Check Order Status**: Track the status of your payment and order.
