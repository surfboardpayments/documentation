Once your merchants receive their terminals, they are ready to be registered and activated. This simple process links the terminal to the merchant and store, enabling you to accept payments. These steps can be completed through two ways

- **Surfboard APIs**: Register the terminal using our API.
- **Partner Portal**: Register the terminal using our web interface.
