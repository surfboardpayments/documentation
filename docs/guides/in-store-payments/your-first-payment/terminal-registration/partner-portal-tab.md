### Prerequisites

- API and Partner Portal access credentials.

### To register a terminal using the Partner Portal

1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, navigate to **Merchants** tab and select the desired merchant.
2. In the merchant's page, go to the **Stores** section and choose the relevant store.
3. Click **Register Terminal**.
4. Select the **Register Physical Terminal** from the dropdown, enter the required details, and complete the terminal registration.
