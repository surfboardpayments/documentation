1. Log in to the Merchant Portal and go to the **Sales** section.
2. Click the transaction you want to refund.
3. Click **Create refund** and then **Initiate refund**.

{% callout type="tip" label="Tip" %}
**Alternative**: In side bar Terminal section, You can **create a refund** for a particular transaction by clicking the arrow in the right corner.
