## Lets Work on Your Post Payment

Congratulations on successfully creating your first payment! Now that the transaction is complete, you can use our post-payment functions to manage it. This guide will walk you through the following operations:

- **Transaction Report**: Downloading the reports of the transactions.
- **Returns/Refunds**: Handling returns for completed orders.
- **Void a payment**: Stopping a payment before it's fully processed.
