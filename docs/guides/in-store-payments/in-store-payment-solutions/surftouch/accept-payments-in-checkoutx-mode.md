In CheckoutX mode, your POS system creates the order, and the terminal handles the payment acceptance.

### **Payment Acceptance Flow**

1. Turn on the device and tap **Accept Payment** to take it to the idle screen.
2. Create the order in your POS system. The SurfTouch displays the order amount and awaits the payment request.
3. The terminal prompts the customer to tap or insert their card.

{% callout type="note" label="Note" %}  
If tips  are enabled, a popup on the SurfTouch prompts customers to choose their preferred tip amount before completing the payment.
{% /callout %}
    
4. **Payment Methods**

| Method | Function | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Contactless (Tap)** | Customer taps their card on the screen. The payment processes, and a success message is displayed. | SurfTouch may occasionally prompt the customer for a **PIN** or to **insert their card**. |
| **Insertion (Chip)** | Customer inserts the card chip-up into the slot at the bottom until it seats firmly. | **DO NOT REMOVE** the card until the payment is fully processed. The customer enters their **PIN** when guided by the on-screen prompts. |
5. After payment, SurfTouch displays a **digital receipt** with a **QR code** for the customer to scan. This feature is **configurable** via the Merchant Dashboard.
