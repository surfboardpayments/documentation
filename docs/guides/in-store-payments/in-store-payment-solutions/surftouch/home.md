The SurfTouch is an all-in-one Android payment acceptance terminal from Surfboard Payments, offering payment versatility and operational efficiency in a compact form factor.

SurfTouch supports:

- Card payments
- Contactless payments
- Multiple connectivity options
- Your own payment apps
- Multiple payment methods
- Digital receipts and much more

## Operating modes

| Mode | Integration | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **CheckoutX** | **Attached (API/App)** | POS system creates the order; SurfTouch only handles the payment processing. |
| **SurfPay** | **Standalone** | SurfTouch manages the entire process: order creation through to payment completion. |
