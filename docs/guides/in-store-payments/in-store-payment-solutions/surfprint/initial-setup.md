The terminal is pre-configured and ready for use with your merchant account upon arrival.

1. Press and hold the **Power button** to turn it on. If it doesn't power up, use the provided USB Type-C cable to charge it.
2. On the welcome screen, tap **Select a WiFi network** and choose your preferred network from the list.
3. A pop-up will ask you to enable Roaming for GSM connectivity. Tap **Enable Roaming** to activate it (no extra cost). This ensures network redundancy.
4. Once connected to the Surfboard servers, tap **Register Terminal**. Use the displayed **6-digit OTP** to complete registration via your Merchant Portal or external software provider.
5. The terminal will enter update mode automatically and install necessary software.
