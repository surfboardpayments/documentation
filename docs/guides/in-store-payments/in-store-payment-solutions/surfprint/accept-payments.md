### **Receipt Troubleshooting**

| Issue | Cause | Troubleshooting Steps |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **No Print / Blank Receipts** | Incorrect paper orientation, open latch, or empty roll. | **Verify** the thermal side is facing up. **Confirm** the latch is securely closed. **Check** the paper roll level. |
| **Faint / Unclear Print** | Incompatible or damaged paper. | **Ensure** you are using 58 mm compatible thermal paper. **Avoid** damp or heat-exposed paper. **Try** a new roll from a verified supplier. |
| **Paper Not Feeding** | Paper jam or debris. | **Open the latch** and carefully clear any jams. **Inspect** the area around the feed rollers for dirt and clean if needed. |
