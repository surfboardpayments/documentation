1. An order is created in your system.
2. A payment is initiated.

![prebuilt app](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/cpayments3.png "checkoutX")

3. The customer can now either tap their card on the terminal or insert their card into the card reader slot, depending on the type of card and the customer's preference.
4. Once the card is read by the terminal, it processes the payment.

![prebuilt app](https://developer-portal-docs.web.app/images/cpayments4.png "checkoutX")

5. After the processing, the terminal will display a success screen, signifying that the payment has been successfully completed.
