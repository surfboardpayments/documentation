1. Turn the device on; the idle screen will display if the SurfPad is registered.
2. Create the order and initiate the card payment request from your external system (POS/ECR). The total amount will display on the SurfPad.
3. The customer taps their card when the first indicator LED turns green. All LEDs turn green upon completion. (Note: May occasionally prompt for PIN or card insertion.)
4. The customer inserts the chip-up until firmly seated. The customer enters the PIN when prompted. **Do not remove the card** until the payment complete screen is displayed to avoid failure.
5. After a successful payment, the SurfPad displays a success screen.
