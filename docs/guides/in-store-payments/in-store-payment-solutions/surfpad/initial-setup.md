1. Ensure all components (terminal, charging cable) are present. Contact support if anything is damaged or missing.
2. Press and hold the **Power ON/OFF key**. Charge via USB Type-C or charging dock if the battery is depleted.
3. **Network Connection**: When the Connection screen appears:
    - **Connect WiFi**: Press **6 2 6 8**, select the network, and enter the password. (Only 2.4GHz bands are supported.)
    - **Connect Mobile (GSM)**: Press **4 7 6 0** to use the inbuilt SIM card.
    
    {% callout type="caution" label="Note" %}
    **Connection Error***:* To troubleshoot, press **9 4 3 4** (reconnect default WiFi), **6 2 6 8** (select new WiFi), or **4 7 6 0** (switch to mobile), or press **Cancel** to force a server connection retry.
    {% /callout %}
    
4. Once connected to Surfboard servers, a registration prompt appears. Use the displayed **Serial Number** (also found on the back) to register the device via the Partner Portal.
5. Long-press the **Power key** to shut down, then power back on. The device will automatically receive required updates and configurations.
