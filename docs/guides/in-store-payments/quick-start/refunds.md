# Refunds

Understand the different refund options available and how to process them for your customers.

## Refund Types Overview

### Full Refunds

Process a complete refund for the entire order amount.

### Partial Refunds

Process a refund for specific items or a custom amount from the original order.

---

## Full Refund Options

### 1. Full Refund with Adjustments (Default)

Refund the entire order including any tips, fees, or adjustments.

**Includes**: Original order amount + tips + fees + adjustments

### 2. Full Refund without Adjustments

When you want to refund only the base order amount, excluding tips and fees.

**Includes**: Original order amount only

---

## Partial Refund Options

### 1. Partial Refund by Order Lines

#### A. With Adjustments (Default)

Refunding specific items while including proportional adjustments.

**Includes**: Selected items + tips/fees

#### B. Without Adjustments

Refunding specific items without including any adjustments.

**Includes**: Selected items only

### 2. Partial Refund by Custom Amount

#### A. With Adjustments (Default)

Refunding a specific dollar amount while including proportional adjustments.

**Includes**: Custom amount + proportional tips/fees

#### B. Without Adjustments

Refunding exactly the specified amount without any additional charges.

**Includes**: Exact custom amount only
