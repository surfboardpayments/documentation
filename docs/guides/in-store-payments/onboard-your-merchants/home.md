## Onboard your Merchants

Congratulations on successfully creating your developer account! As a Surfboard partner, you now have two straightforward options for onboarding your merchants.

1. **Via Surfboard APIs**: Create merchant applications through our APIs.
2. **Via Partner Portal**: Create merchant applications manually through our web interface

Both methods generate a unique merchant application URL which you should share with your merchant so they can complete the application and submit it.
