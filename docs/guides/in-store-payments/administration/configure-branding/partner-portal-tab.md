**For Merchant-Level Configuration**

1. Merchant-level settings are configured by navigating to **Merchants→ select the desired merchant→ Configurations.**
2. Goto **Set Merchant Branding Config,** fill in the details, and click **Save Changes** to apply.

**For Partner-Level Configuration**

1. Partners can configure the terminals at their level by navigating through **Settings→** **Set Partner Branding Config,** enter the required terminal configs, and click **Save Changes.**
