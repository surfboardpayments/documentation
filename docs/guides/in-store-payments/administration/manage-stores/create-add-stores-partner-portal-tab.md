description: "Guide to create and add stores through Partner Portal."
category: "In-Store Payment Guides"
path: "/guides/in-store-payments/administration/manage-stores/create-add-stores-partner-portal-tab.md"
---

1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, go to **Merchants**, and select the desired merchant.
2. In the **Stores** section, click **Create Store**, fill in the required details, and submit to add a new store under the selected merchant.
