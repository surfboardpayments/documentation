---
title: "Manage Update Terminal Name using Partner Portal"
description: "Guide to managing and configuring update terminal name using Partner Portal."
category: "Terminals"
path: "/guides/in-store-payments/administration/manage-terminals/additional-functions/update-terminal-name-partner-portal-tab.md"
---

1. Login to the **Partner Portal**, goto **Terminal Info** tab.
2. Enter the terminalId or serial number of the terminal to be named, click **Get Terminal Details**.
3. Click the edit icon. In the pop-up sheet on the right, enter the **Terminal Name** and click **Update Name**.
