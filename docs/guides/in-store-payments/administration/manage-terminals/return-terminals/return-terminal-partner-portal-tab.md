---
title: "Returning Malfunction/Defective Terminal Using Partner Portal"
description: "Guide to returning payment terminals using the Partner Portal."
category: "Terminals"
path: "/guides/in-store-payments/administration//manage-terminals/return-terminals/return-terminal-partner-portal-tab.md"
---

1. Log into the **Partner Portal**, go to **Terminal Info**, enter the **Terminal ID** (obtained from terminal registration) or **Serial Number** (located on the back of the hardware terminal), and click **Get Terminal Details** to view terminal info.
2. Select **Return Order** in the top-right corner to open the return form. 
3. Fill in the required details and click **Return Order**.
