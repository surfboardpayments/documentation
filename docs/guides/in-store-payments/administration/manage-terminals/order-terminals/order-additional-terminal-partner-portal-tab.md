If a terminal was not ordered during onboarding, or if the merchant requires additional terminals, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Merchants** tab, select the merchant, and navigate to the **Order Hardware** section.
2. Fill in the details and choose the **Product** and **Quantity** to be shipped. Click **Create Shipment**.
