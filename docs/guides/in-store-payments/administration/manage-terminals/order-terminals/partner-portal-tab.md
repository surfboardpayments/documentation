title: "Order Terminals Using Partner Portal"
description: "Step-by-step guide to order terminals through the Partner Portal."
category: "In-Store Payments Guides"
path: "/guides/in-store-payments/administration//manage-terminals/order-terminals/partner-portal-tab.md"
---
1. Log in to the **Partner portal**, navigate to the **Applications** in dashboard, and select **Create Application**.
2. Fill in the mandatory details, activate the **Show Webshop** toggle for terminal ordering, and click **Create Application**, a **WebKYB URL** will be sent to the merchant to complete the application.
