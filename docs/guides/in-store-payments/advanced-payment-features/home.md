# Advanced Payment Features

You can leverage customizable payment features through our terminals. This integration allows merchants to accept payments for goods and services while helping partners expand their offerings and add value. Advanced payment features include:

- Partial payments
- Return/Refund an partial payments
- Customer Identification on Card Tap
- Payment methods
- Cancel a payment
- Payment status
- Capture a payment
