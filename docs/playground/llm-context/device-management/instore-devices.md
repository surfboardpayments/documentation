### Guide

# Device Management

Once your merchants receive their devices, they are ready to be registered and activated. This simple process links the device to the merchant and store, enabling you to accept payments. These steps can be completed

#### Using API

1. Power on the terminal after receiving it. A 6-digit code (**registrationId**) will be displayed.(for SurfPad/Printer, use the serial number on the back of the device)
2. Enter the **`registrationIdentiifer`** and the **`storeId`** (the terminal where it will be registered).

{% callout type="info" label="Info" %}
For softPOS solutions, registration can also be done via inter-app switch if configured on your end. See the Inter-App Guide for details.
{% /callout %}

#### Using Partner Portal

1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, navigate to **Merchants** tab and select the desired merchant.
2. In the merchant's page, go to the **Stores** section and choose the relevant store.
3. Click **Register Terminal**, Enter the **`registrationIdentiifer`** and the **`storeId`** (the terminal where it will be registered).

#### Using Merchant Portal

1. Login to the merchant portal, go to **Stores** section in the side bar, simply click the required store you want to register the terminal.
2. Goto **Terminals** section, click **Register New terminal**, Enter the **`registrationIdentiifer`** and the **`storeId`** (the terminal where it will be registered).
