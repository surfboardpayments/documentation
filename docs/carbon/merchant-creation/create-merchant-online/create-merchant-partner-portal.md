As a Surfboard partner, you can also onboard merchants directly through the Partner Portal. This method allows you to enter merchant details yourself and create merchants without using APIs. Once the information is submitted, the merchant record is created and onboarding continues through the portal.

To generate an onboarding link

1. Log in to your **Partner Portal** account.
2. Navigate to the **Applications** section.
3. Enable the toggle to enable online payments for your store at the bottom and fill in the mandatory fields.
4. Click **Create Application**. A **`webKybUrl`** will be generated to share with the merchant.
