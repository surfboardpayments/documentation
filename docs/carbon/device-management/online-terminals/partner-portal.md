1. Log in to the **Partner Portal**, navigate to **Merchants** tab and select the desired merchant.
2. In the merchant's page, go to the **Stores** section and choose the relevant store.
3. Click **Register Online Terminal**, enter the **`onlineTerminalMode`**.

> You only need to register the **`SelfHostedPage`** mode; the other modes, **`PaymentPage`** and **`MerchantInitiated`**, are registered automatically after the merchant completes their application.
