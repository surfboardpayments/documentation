# In-store Terminals

This page provides an overview of the available in-store terminals (both hardware and SoftPOS) from Surfboard Payments and their typical use cases.

## Available Terminals

### **SurfTouch**

All-in-one Android touchscreen payment terminal designed for countertop and integrated POS environments.

**Hardware Features:** Large touchscreen display, integrated contactless and chip card reader,  receipt printer as an add-on, camera, and multiple ports for accessories.

**Connectivity:** WiFi, GSM

**Supports:** Custom apps, seamless integration with POS systems, robust security features, and digital receipt capabilities.

**Use Cases:** Retail checkout, restaurants, integrated POS environments.

### **SurfPad**

Handheld payment terminal with integrated card reader and physical keypad, optimized for mobility.

**Hardware Features:** Compact design, physical keypad, contactless and chip card reader, indicator LEDs.

**Connectivity:** WiFi, Cellular data.

**Unique Advantages:** Lightweight and portable, ideal for on-the-go payments, easy to use in busy or mobile environments.

**Use Cases:** Table-side ordering, queue busting, mobile sales, delivery, events.

### **SurfPrint**

SurfPrint is functionally identical to SurfTouch in terms of setup and operation, with the key difference being the inclusion of a dedicated printer for imminent receipt or ticket printing. It is designed for seamless integration with POS systems and where immediate physical receipts are required.

**Hardware Features:** Thermal printer, robust build for high-volume environments, integrated contactless and chip card reader.

**Connectivity:** WiFi, cellular data.

**Unique Advantages:** All SurfTouch features plus imminent printing capability, ideal for environments where printed receipts or tickets are mandatory.

**Use Cases:** High-volume receipt printing, ticketing at events or transportation, kiosk integrations, retail environments requiring instant printouts.

### **CheckoutX (SoftPOS)**

CheckoutX is Surfboard Payments' pre-built payment acceptance application, available as a SoftPOS solution for compatible Android devices (NFC-enabled) and pre-installed on SurfTouch and SurfPrint. It transforms Android devices into PCI-certified payment terminals for contactless card acceptance.

**Modes:**

- **Attached Mode (CheckoutX):** CheckoutX is integrated with your POS system (typically on SurfTouch or SurfPrint), where the POS creates the order and CheckoutX handles payment processing.
- **Standalone Mode (SoftPOS):** CheckoutX runs independently on an Android device, handling both order creation and payment acceptance directly within the app.

**Hardware Features:** Utilizes device's NFC and security modules; no additional hardware required for SoftPOS mode.

**Connectivity:** Uses device's WiFi or mobile data.

**Unique Advantages:** No hardware investment required for SoftPOS, rapid deployment, flexible integration (attached or standalone), digital receipts, and customizable branding.

**Use Cases:** Mobile businesses, pop-up shops, delivery services, events, retail, restaurants, businesses seeking a hardware-free or integrated payment solution.
