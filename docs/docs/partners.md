# Partners

Surfboard partners with Independent Software Vendors (ISVs), payment facilitators, and institutional customers seeking to provide payment solutions seamlessly alongside their existing products.

We offer both online and in-store payment solution where we offer a suite of white-label products ready for immediate use. Partners can build and integrate with various solutions, including our pre-built checkout, hardware terminals, and CPOC implementation to address diverse payment scenarios. Additionally, our SDKs and APIs give you the flexibility to create unique solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Payment institutions can also partner with Surfboard to utilise our hardware terminals and software solutions, operating under their existing payment licenses.

## Partner capabilities

Partners will be given access to the Surfboard Partner Portal, allowing you to manage your merchants, configure settings for them, and access many more functionalities.

A partner will be able to:

-   [**Onboard merchants**](/guides/in-store-payments/create-merchant-and-store-setup/home)
-   [**Provide terminals and accessories for the merchants**](/docs/hardware-terminals)
-   [**Register terminals**](/guides/in-store-payments/terminal-registration/home) and [**initiate payments**](/guides/in-store-payments/accept-payments/initiate-payment) through APIs on behalf of the merchant
-   [**Fetch and display all merchant transactions**](/api/transactions)
-   Use our receipt host for the digital distribution of receipts
   
## Getting started with Surfboard as a partner

To get started with Surfboard as a partner, you first need to create a developer account through the Surfboard Console. During the signup process, you will be required to provide relevant information as prompted. Once you complete the signup and verification steps, you will be onboarded and initiated to proceed as a Surfboard partner, gaining access to the Partner Portal and its functionalities.

## Partner Types

- **ISO Partner:**  
  Partners without a financial license. Surfboard handles merchant onboarding, payment processing, and settlements.

- **FI Partner (PF Partner):**  
  Partners with a financial license. Surfboard only provides the platform for their use. The partner is responsible for onboarding, processing, and settlements.


## Required Information for In-Store Payments

When setting up in-store payments, ensure you have the following information:
- **Acquirer MID**
- **PF MID** (for the payment institution, if applicable)
- **MCC Code**

## Configuration options for partners

As a partner you can also configure several settings for you merchants from the Partner Portal or using the respective [**APIs**](/guides/in-store-payments/integrations/api). The configuration settings include, but not limited to:

-   [**Hardware Terminals and accessories**](/docs/hardware-terminals) that are available for purchase
-   Branding for terminals and KYB Screens
-   Currency codes and operating countries
-   Other configuration options like system updates, sleep time, tipping, currencies etc.

> To get started as a partner, please contact us at integration@surfboard.se.
