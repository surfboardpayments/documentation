# Transactions

In Surfboard, transactions are the definitive records for all payments that are completed. Whenever a payment is completed, an associated transaction record is created.

Each transaction is identified by a unique **Transaction ID**. This ID is essential for tracking, auditing, and for providing customer support.

Transaction records enable merchants to:

-   Perform financial auditing to ensure accuracy in accounting.
-   Assist in dispute resolution, where the **Transaction ID** serves as the first step in investigating issues.
-   Facilitate the tracking and reconciliation of partial payments for individual orders.
-   Gain insights into customer purchase behavior for campaigns and promotions.
-   Enable easier reconciliation after settlements, streamlining the financial close process.
-   Monitor transaction volumes over specific periods for resource planning.

## Transaction reports

To give you a broader and organized view of your transactions, we offer [**Transaction reports**](/docs/reporting). These reports are generated daily, with a cutoff time of 23:59 CET, and are sent directly to you.

Transaction reports are highly detailed, capturing both customer information, and a complete list of transactions with timestamps, reference numbers, amounts, and indicative fees.
