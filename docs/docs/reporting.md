# Reporting

Surfboard provides a versatile reporting system that enables merchants to gain insights into various aspects of their operations. Our reports equip you with all the necessary data to make informed decisions for your business. With our precise reporting, merchants can:

-   Track revenue
-   Monitor transaction volumes
-   Identify fraud
-   Reconcile financial records and much more.

## Report types

Surfboard provides two types of reports to merchants.

### 1. Transaction reports

Transaction reports offer a comprehensive breakdown of all the transactions received by the merchant on the previous day with a cutoff at 23:59 CET. Transaction reports are generated daily and sent to the merchants at night.

A transaction report has the following information:

-   **Customer Information:** This section of the transaction report includes details about the customer, merchant ID, settlement currency, and transaction dates.
-   **Transaction list:** This section of the transaction report provides a detailed list covering the previous day's transactions, including the transaction time, transaction reference, card number, amount, and indicative fees.

### 2. Settlement reports

Settlement reports offer a comprehensive breakdown of all customer payments received by Surfboard and settled to your merchant account. Daily settlement reports covering the prior day's transactions are sent to the merchants registered email address at 16:00 CET.

A Settlement report has the following information:

-   **Customer Information:** This section of the settlement report includes details about the customer, merchant ID, Settlement currency.
-   **Payout information:** This section of the settlement report contains daily settlement summary, such as the reference number, time of the payout, fees and deductions, payout amount, and pending liabilities.
-   **Transaction list:** This section of the settlement report provides a detailed list of all transactions included in the settlement, including the transaction reference, amount, card brand, commission, and payout amount.
-   **Charges:** This section of the settlement report provides a detailed list of all charges such as pending liability, subscription fees, hardware costs or dispute charges listed. The charges section of the report appears only if any charges are incurred during that specific settlement run.
